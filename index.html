MY STORY

💻 About ROMA-TECH 🇺🇬

I'm ROMA-TECH, a Ugandan developer, web coder and WhatsApp bot creator. I was born on 24 November 2005 and grew up in Kitigoma, Uganda.

My journey into technology was not easy. Financial challenges affected my path to university, but coding remained my dream. I continued learning through practice, research, mistakes, testing and building projects.

While working and building an independent life, I use my free time to explore JavaScript, Node.js, web development and WhatsApp bot development.

“It was not easy, but the good thing is — I have it.” — ROMA-TECH

🤖 Creator of MUFASER-X 🚀

On 23 May 2026, I created MUFASER-X, a WhatsApp bot project. For me, MUFASER-X represents my dream, struggle, learning journey and determination to build something of my own.
